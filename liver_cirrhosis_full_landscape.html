The clinical reality nobody talks about — Cirrhosis is silent until it's too late. By the time symptoms appear (jaundice, ascites, bleeding), the liver has already lost 70–80% of function. 1.48 million people died from cirrhosis in 2019 alone — an 8.1% increase from 2017. The current gold standard diagnosis is a liver biopsy — a needle through your abdomen, prone to sampling error, expensive, painful, and unavailable in most of India.

What it does
Binary + staging classification on T2-MRI. VGG16 + MobileNet + DenseNet121 ensemble. 96.92% accuracy, 98.93% recall. Uses Grad-CAM for basic XAI. 10-fold cross-validation.
What it doesn't do
No blood marker integration. No low-resource ultrasound. No Indian data. No progression prediction. No SHAP on clinical features. All four of these are YOUR contribution.
